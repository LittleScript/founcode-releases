# Usage Policy — Founcode

*Last updated: July 7, 2026*

Founcode orchestrates powerful AI coding agents. Use them responsibly.

**You may not use Founcode to:**

- Create, deploy, or distribute malware, exploits, or code designed to damage, disrupt, or gain unauthorized access to systems or data.
- Work on codebases you do not own or lack authorization to modify.
- Violate the acceptable-use policies of the AI providers behind your agents (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, DeepSeek, and others) — their policies apply to everything their models produce through Founcode.
- Circumvent Founcode's licensing, or resell access to the application.

**Good practice we encourage:**

- Review every diff before merging — that's what the Verify gate is for.
- Keep secrets out of prompts and repositories.
- Use version control (Founcode assumes git) and keep backups.

Violations may result in license revocation. Report abuse or security issues via [GitHub issues](https://github.com/LittleScript/founcode-releases/issues).

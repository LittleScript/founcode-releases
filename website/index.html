THE PIPELINE

Five gates between an idea
and your branch.

01

Blueprint

Describe what you want in plain words. Founcode interviews you, drafts a structure map and a full PRD, then decomposes it into an ordered task graph.

02

Plan

The agent analyzes your repo read-only and drafts a reviewable plan. Nothing is written until you approve it.

03

Execute

Work happens in an isolated git worktree. Your branch, your staged changes, your flow — never touched mid-flight.

04

Verify

A separate agent run checks the work against the plan and runs your build & tests. Failures loop back automatically — capped, never unattended.

05

Merge

One click — only after verification passed and you reviewed the diff. The worktree is cleaned. No junk branches, ever.
